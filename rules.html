BDMC Official Rules

Last updated: 5 August 2025

Welcome to the official rules page for BDMC. Here you will find our network-wide guidelines and Discord server rules. Should you fail to abide by these rules, you may lose the ability to participate in our community. Please keep in mind at all times that members of our Staff team can issue disciplinary actions for offences not listed here, and that you are solely responsible for the security and integrity of your own account. If you wish to report a possible rule violation, please visit our Support server on Discord using the link below.

We hope you enjoy a safe and comfortable experience on our server. Thank you!
- BDMC Management and Staff
